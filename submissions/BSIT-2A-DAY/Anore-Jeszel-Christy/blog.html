My Journey as a Student

Being a student is more than attending classes or passing exams; it’s a journey of growth and self-discovery. Each day brings challenges, from tough subjects to group projects, but every step teaches me resilience and patience.

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”

Musics that Inspires Me

Music inspires me because it speaks to emotions words often cannot. A song can lift my mood, calm my mind, or make me feel understood in ways nothing else can. Listening to heartfelt tracks like Sa Bawat Sandali reminds me of love, hope, and perseverance.

My all-time favorite songs are:

  • Tenerife sea by: Ed Sheeran
  • Sa bawat sandali by: Amiel Sol
  • Ordinary Song by: Marc Velasco
“Where words fail, music speaks.”
