Why I Want to Learn to Code

September 25, 2025

I want to learn to code because it helps me create solutions, bring ideas to life, and solve real-world problems. It also strengthens my logical thinking and problem-solving skills, which are essential in today’s technology-driven world.
As Bill Gates once said:

“Learning to write programs stretches your mind and helps you think better, creating a way of thinking about things that I think is helpful in all domains.”

My Life in College

September 15, 2025

My life in college as a BSIT student is both challenging and rewarding. I spend my days learning about technology, coding, and solving problems while improving my skills. Balancing projects and studies can be tough, but it helps me grow and prepares me for a future in the tech industry.
