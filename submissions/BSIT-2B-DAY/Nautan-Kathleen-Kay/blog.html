My Favorite Place to Relax

September 28, 2025

Whenever I need peace and quiet, I go to my secret spot near the ocean. It's quiet, breezy, and perfect for drawing. Sunsets truly heal.

“We can find peace in silence.”

Here's what I usually bring with me:

  • Sketchbook
  • Colored pencils
  • Portable speaker
  • A journal for thoughts

I often say, All I need is a breeze and a blank page.

Anime helped me express myself confidently. It teaches values like friendship, perseverance, and empathy.
