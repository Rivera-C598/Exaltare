Max Verstappen: The Dominant Force in F1

September 25, 2025

Max Verstappen, a Dutch racing driver for Red Bull Racing, has become one of the most dominant figures in modern Formula 1. Known for his aggressive driving style and incredible consistency, Verstappen has redefined what it means to be a champion in motorsport.

Since making his debut at just 17 years old, he has broken multiple records, including being the youngest driver to win a Grand Prix. His recent seasons have shown near-total dominance, often finishing far ahead of the competition. Fans admire his fearlessness, while rivals respect his precision on track.

“Once you are in front, you just want to push harder and harder.” – Max Verstappen

My Journey and Dreams

September 25, 2025

Since childhood, I have always dreamed of becoming an IT professional. Choosing BSIT in college was not just a random decision—it was a path I had envisioned from the start.

Now, as I turn 21 this coming October 22(already 21 lol), I’ve noticed changes in myself. I play fewer games and focus more on enhancing my skills, gaining new knowledge, and surrounding myself with people who can guide me toward a better future.

Life has given me many challenges, and I treat them as part of my growth. Even when survival feels hard, we must keep showing up and never give up.

In the next five years, I see myself in a stable job or building my own business — or both.

Don’t think too much about the future. Explore new things that can benefit your career. Always be kind, stay humble, and live the life you truly want.

“If your plan doesn't work, change the plan — not the goal.” – Max Verstappen
